# Stormy 9 - A Macropad Designed to be Simple
## In today's age of mechanical keyboards, people want simple and customizable. The Stormy 9 allows by having up to 16 programmable buttons, a knob to adjust volume and brightness, and an OLED display showing off your WPM and a cute picture of my cat (non-negotiable).

### * 9 MX-style mechanical switches
### * 1 EC11 rotary encoder
### * 1 0.91" 128X32 OLED display
### * 9 Blank DSA keycaps
### * 4 M3x16mm screws
### * 4 M3x5x4mm heatset inserts
